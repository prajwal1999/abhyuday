FAQs
How do I avail accommodation during the Fest?
Just choose ‘Yes’ in the accommodation option provided while registerin
Is accomodation free for the Fest?
No. We will take accommodation charges from you when you arrive in the institute.
What is the delegate programme?
Delegates from many colleges/NGO’s/organisations are invited to attend the Annual Social Festival. To get an invite, contact the Media and PR Managers
What is the maximum number of delegates can one bring from their college/NGO?
There is no upper limit to the number of delegates than can be brought to the Fest.
Who all can attend the Annual Social Festival?
The Annual Social Festival is open to everyone and is not restricted to any particular audience.
My NGO does not work on this year’s theme ‘Human Rights’. Can I still attend the Fest?
Yes Indeed! Our motto for this Fest to spread awareness and create impact on Human Rights, and we want to do it as much as possible. So everyone is invited!
